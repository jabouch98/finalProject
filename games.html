Games!


Below is a list of games that the game club has.


Monopoly

A board game where players buy and trade properties. The last person with money is the winner. You can play with up to six players.

Yahtzee

A game in which you roll five dice in order to make certain combinations to score points.

Scrabble

A game in which players make words by placing letter tiles on a board with a grid of squares to score points.

Risk

A game in which players must roll dice to take over territories that opponents have control over. The person who takes over the whole board wins.

Settlers of Catan

A game in which players collect and trade resources in order to built and buy items to gain victory points. The first person to ten wins.

Pictionary

A game in which players play in teams and their teammates must quess what they are drawing in order to advance around the board.

Pandemic

A co-op game in which players must cure four diseases and stop them from spreading.

Sorry

A game in which the players must get their four pieces to their home before the other players by drawing cards that determine movement.
