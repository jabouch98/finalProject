Welcome!


The URI Board Game Club is a child club of the Game Club. We meet once a week on Thursday nights at 6:00pm. We do not end at a certain time. We just play board games until we get tired. However, we are done most nights by 10:00pm or 11:00pm. The Board Game Club provides a few games, although you are encouraged to bring your own. The Board Game Club is not a competitive club. We play to have fun. We usually have a different room each week. However, if you come by the game club room in the Union, you can find out the room number. We welcome any new members and you don't need to sign-up for anything. Please stop on by!
